Luosifen

What is a Luosifen?

Allergy: Peanut

The Luosifen, located in Southern China. The flavor can be described as fresh, fragrant, sour, spicy, and refreshing. The noodles are then topped with sour bamboo shoots, peanuts, fried bean curd, boy choy, wood ear, and capers. The noodles are made with green food rich in nutrients and high in protein and calcium, while still being low in fat. The soup is sour and spicy flavor.

What ingredient you need?

  • Rice vermicelli
  • Sour bamboo shoots
  • Capers
  • Wood ear
  • Bok choy
  • Peanuts
  • Fried bean curd
  • Chili pepper

How to make a Luosifen?

  • You boil the noodles.
  • Add the ingredients of the soup base packet to boiling water.
  • Add the boiled noodles and simmer until it is soft.
  • You can add whatever extra ingredients you want.
